## 📊 Key Findings from Analysis

### Performance Trends
1. **Below Industry Standard**: The average retention rate of **71.9%** falls significantly short of the industry target of 85%
2. **Quarterly Volatility**: Performance shows inconsistency with a 9.08% swing between Q1 (lowest) and Q2 (highest)
3. **Recovery Pattern**: Strong recovery in Q2 followed by slight decline in Q3 and partial recovery in Q4
4. **Q1 Challenge**: First quarter consistently underperforms, suggesting seasonal or onboarding issues

### Statistical Insights
- **Standard Deviation**: 4.12% indicating moderate variability
- **Trend Direction**: Overall positive with 3 of 4 quarters above the annual average
- **Best Quarter**: Q2 2024 at 74.85% (closest to target)
- **Improvement Needed**: 13.1 percentage points to reach industry benchmark
